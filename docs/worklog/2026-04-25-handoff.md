### Architectural state

The graft engine's parts are all individually proven and unit-tested in isolation. What's still required to ship the engine itself:

- **Content-stream emission** for shapes (rect/line/ellipse/circle/path) and text (with tspan + per-char x-arrays). This is the new code that translates SVG primitives into raw PDF operator strings, parallel to `pdfExport.ts` but emitting strings instead of pdf-lib calls. Two beads: `uuz` and `e1j`.
- **Page-resource font registration + content-stream append** primitives mirroring `scripts/spike/02b-embed-new-font.mjs` and the second half of `02-graft-overlay.mjs`. One bead: `ne4`.
- **Engine entry point** that composes everything (classifier ŌåÆ graft / overlay / mixed dispatch ŌåÆ primitive emission ŌåÆ save). Bead `hnj`.

Once those four sub-beads land, `wjj` itself closes (it depends on `hnj`), then `u7r` wires the engine into `fileio.ts` `exportPdf` (replaces or augments the pdf-lib path), then `6d0` is a real-flyer byte-diff round-trip test (the gate that proves Phase 2 actually works on real-world content).
